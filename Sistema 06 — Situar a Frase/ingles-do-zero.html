MÓDULO 2 — VERBOS COM PRONOMES (A1)

O padrão: verbo com 6 pronomes — I / We / You / They / He / She
(He/She adiciona -s no presente simples)

VerboIWeYouTheyHeShe
driveI drive to work.We drive together.You drive well.They drive fast.He drives a taxi.She drives every day.

runI run every day.We run in the park.You run fast.They run together.He runs in the morning.She runs after work.
eatI eat breakfast.We eat together.You eat slowly.They eat at home.He eats a lot.She eats salad.
drinkI drink water.We drink coffee.You drink tea.They drink juice.He drinks milk.She drinks water.
workI work every day.We work together.You work hard.They work online.He works at night.She works from home.
studyI study English.We study together.You study well.They study a lot.He studies hard.She studies every day.
speakI speak English.We speak slowly.You speak well.They speak fast.He speaks clearly.She speaks softly.
liveI live here.We live together.You live far.They live nearby.He lives alone.She lives with family.

Regra -s no He/She/It:

  • verbo normal → + s: work → works, eat → eats, live → lives
  • terminado em -sh/-ch/-x → + es: watch → watches
  • terminado em consonante + y → ies: study → studies
