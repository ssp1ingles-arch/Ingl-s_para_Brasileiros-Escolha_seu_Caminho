Conectar Frases

Perguntar, combinar palavras e soar natural. Três frentes que transformam frases soltas em conversa de verdade — das perguntas do dia a dia às expressões que nativos realmente usam.

💬 Conversa 01 — Rotina diária de trabalho · What / How / When
What time do you get up?
I get up at around 6 a.m.
How do you get to work?
I usually walk to work.
When does your work day start?
It starts at 9:00 a.m.
How long is your lunch break?
It's about 1 hour long.
When do you finish work?
I finish at 5:00 p.m.
