CONVERSA 01 — ROTINA DIÁRIA DE TRABALHO

Tema: Trabalho, horários, rotina
Nível: Iniciante
Question words: What / How / When

Q: What time do you get up?
A: I get up at around 6 a.m.

Q: How do you get to work?
A: I usually walk to work.

Q: When does your work day start?
A: It starts at 9:00 a.m.

Q: What do you do during lunch?
A: I eat at the office cafeteria.

Q: How long is your lunch break?
A: It's about 1 hour long.

Q: When do you finish work?
A: I finish at 5:00 p.m.

Q: What do you do in the evening?
A: I watch YouTube videos and relax.

VOCABULÁRIO:

  • get up = acordar / levantar
  • walk to work = ir ao trabalho a pé
  • work day = dia de trabalho
  • lunch break = pausa para almoço
  • cafeteria = cantina / refeitório
  • in the evening = à noite / no período da tarde/noite

FRASES-CHAVE:

  1. "I get up at around ___." → Acordo por volta das ___.
  2. "I usually walk to ___." → Geralmente vou a pé para ___.
  3. "It starts at ___." → Começa às ___.
  4. "It's about ___ long." → Dura cerca de ___.

PRATIQUE FALANDO SOBRE VOCÊ:

  • What time do you get up? → I get up at ___.
  • How do you get to work/school? → I ___ to ___.
  • What do you do in the evening? → I ___.

ANÁLISE DE QUESTION WORDS:

  • WHAT = o quê / qual (objeto, atividade)
  • HOW = como (maneira, modo)
  • WHEN = quando (tempo)
  • HOW LONG = quanto tempo (duração)

CONVERSA 02 — CIDADE NATAL

Tema: Origem, cidade natal, saudades
Nível: Iniciante
Question words: Where / How / What / Do / Will

Q: Where are you from originally?
A: I'm from a small town in Mexico.

Q: How big is your hometown?
A: It has about 50,000 people.

Q: What is your hometown known for?
A: It's known for its colorful festivals.

Q: How is it different from this city?
A: It's much quieter and slower paced.

Q: What do people do there for fun?
A: They go to the plaza on weekends.

Q: Do you miss your hometown?
A: Yes, I miss it very much.

Q: Will you go back someday?
A: Yes, I hope to go back soon.

VOCABULÁRIO:

  • originally = originalmente / de origem
  • hometown = cidade natal
  • known for = conhecido por / famoso por
  • slower paced = ritmo mais lento / pace mais tranquilo
  • plaza = praça / centro
  • miss = sentir falta de
  • someday = algum dia

FRASES-CHAVE:

  1. "I'm from a small town in ___." → Sou de uma cidade pequena em ___.
  2. "It's known for its ___." → É conhecida por seu/sua ___.
  3. "It's much quieter and slower paced." → É muito mais quieto e com ritmo mais lento.
  4. "I miss it very much." → Sinto muita falta.
  5. "I hope to go back soon." → Espero voltar em breve.

ERRO CLÁSSICO:
❌ "I miss my city." (correto mas incompleto)
✓ "I miss it very much." (mais natural — usa pronome)
✓ "I really miss my hometown." (mais expressivo)

PRATIQUE COM SUA PRÓPRIA HISTÓRIA:

  • Where are you from originally? → I'm from ___ in ___.
  • What is your hometown known for? → It's known for its ___.
  • Do you miss your hometown? → Yes, I miss ___ / Not really, because ___.

CONVERSA 03 — FILMES E CINEMA

Tema: Filmes, preferências, recomendações
Nível: Iniciante-Intermediário
Question words: What / Why / Who / How often / Was / Would

Q: What kind of movies do you like?
A: I love thrillers and science fiction.

Q: Why do you like science fiction?
A: I love imagining life in the future.

Q: Who is your favorite actor?
A: I really like Cate Blanchett's work.

Q: How often do you go to the cinema?
A: I go about twice a month.

Q: What was the last movie you saw?
A: I saw an action film last weekend.

Q: Was it good?
A: It was okay. Not the best.

Q: What would you recommend watching?
A: You should watch that new Spanish series.

VOCABULÁRIO:

  • thrillers = filmes de suspense
  • science fiction = ficção científica
  • imagining = imaginando
  • favorite actor = ator favorito
  • cinema = cinema (o local)
  • action film = filme de ação
  • recommend = recomendar
  • series = série

FRASES-CHAVE:

  1. "What kind of movies do you like?" → Que tipo de filme você gosta?
  2. "I love imagining ___." → Adoro imaginar ___.
  3. "I go about ___ times a month." → Vou cerca de ___ vezes por mês.
  4. "It was okay. Not the best." → Estava ok. Não o melhor.
  5. "You should watch ___." → Você deveria assistir ___.

COMO RECOMENDAR EM INGLÊS:

  • "You should watch ___." → Você deveria assistir ___.
  • "I highly recommend ___." → Recomendo muito ___.
  • "You'd love ___." → Você adoraria ___.
  • "Don't miss ___." → Não perca ___.

Q: When did you buy it?

Q: Would you recommend it to me?

  • every weekend = todo fim de semana
  • bedroom = quarto
  • older brother = irmão mais velho

  • completely = completamente

  1. "What do you do for fun?" → O que você faz para se divertir?
  2. "I like to ___." → Gosto de ___.
  3. "I play with my ___." → Jogo com meu/minha ___.
  4. "Because it has an amazing ___." → Porque tem um/uma ___ incrível.
  5. "You would love it." → Você adoraria.

PRATIQUE:

  • What do you do for fun? → I like to ___.
  • How often do you ___? → I ___ every ___.
  • Why do you like ___? → Because it ___.

CONVERSA 05 — RESTAURANTE

Tema: Pedir comida, pagar, serviços
Nível: Iniciante
Question words: Can / Do / How about / When / Where / Can we

Q: Can I have a cheeseburger and fries?
A: Sure. Anything else?

Q: Do you serve sushi here?
A: Sorry, we don't have sushi.

Q: How about steak?
A: Yes, we do serve steaks.

Q: When will our food arrive?
A: It will arrive in 15 minutes.

Q: Where is the restroom located?
A: It is down the hallway.

Q: Can we have the bill, please?
A: Right. Yes, I will bring it now.

Q: Can we pay by card?
A: Sorry, we only accept cash.

VOCABULÁRIO:

  • cheeseburger = hambúrguer com queijo
  • fries = batatas fritas
  • Anything else? = Mais alguma coisa?
  • serve = servir / ter no cardápio
  • restroom = banheiro (americano)
  • located = localizado
  • hallway = corredor
  • bill = conta
  • accept cash = aceitar dinheiro

FRASES-CHAVE:

  1. "Can I have ___?" → Posso ter ___? / Me traz ___.
  2. "Do you serve ___ here?" → Vocês servem ___ aqui?
  3. "It will arrive in ___ minutes." → Chegará em ___ minutos.
  4. "It is down the hallway." → É pelo corredor.
  5. "We only accept cash." → Só aceitamos dinheiro.

NO RESTAURANTE — FRASES ESSENCIAIS:

  • "Can I see the menu?" → Posso ver o cardápio?
  • "What do you recommend?" → O que você recomenda?
  • "Can we have the bill?" → Pode trazer a conta?
  • "Is service included?" → A gorjeta está incluída?

ERRO CLÁSSICO:
❌ "Give me a cheeseburger." (rude)
✓ "Can I have a cheeseburger, please?" (educado)
✓ "I'd like a cheeseburger, please." (mais formal)

CONVERSA 06 — CELULAR E TECNOLOGIA

Tema: Smartphones, tecnologia, compras online
Nível: Iniciante-Intermediário
Question words: What / How long / Where / Why / How often / Can / When

Q: What phone do you have?
A: I have the latest iPhone model.

Q: How long does the battery last?
A: It lasts for a whole day.

Q: Where did you buy it?
A: I bought it online last week.

Q: Why is it so expensive?
A: It has an excellent camera system.

Q: How often do you change phones?
A: I change them every 2 years.

Q: Can it take photos underwater?
A: Yes, it is fully waterproof.

Q: When will you upgrade it again?
A: Probably in two or 3 years.

VOCABULÁRIO:

  • latest model = modelo mais recente
  • battery = bateria
  • last = durar (tempo)
  • whole day = dia inteiro
  • camera system = sistema de câmera
  • waterproof = à prova d'água
  • upgrade = atualizar / trocar por modelo melhor

FRASES-CHAVE:

  1. "I have the latest ___." → Tenho o/a ___ mais recente.
  2. "It lasts for a whole day." → Dura o dia inteiro.
  3. "I bought it online." → Comprei online.
  4. "It is fully waterproof." → É totalmente à prova d'água.
  5. "Probably in ___ years." → Provavelmente em ___ anos.

COMO FALAR DE TECNOLOGIA:

  • "It has great features." → Tem ótimos recursos.
  • "The battery life is excellent." → A duração da bateria é excelente.
  • "It's worth the price." → Vale o preço.
  • "I'd highly recommend it." → Recomendo muito.

CONVERSA 07 — LIMPEZA DA CASA

Tema: Casa, limpeza, tarefas domésticas
Nível: Iniciante
Question words: Why / When / Where / How can / Should / What / How long

Q: Why is the kitchen so messy?
A: We cooked a big dinner yesterday.

Q: When will you clean it up?
A: I will clean it right now.

Q: Where is the vacuum cleaner?
A: It is in the hall closet.

Q: How can I help you clean?
A: You can wash the dirty dishes.

Q: Should we wash the windows today?
A: No, it will rain this afternoon.

Q: What did you do with the trash?
A: I put it outside already.

Q: How long will this take?
A: It will take 30 minutes.

VOCABULÁRIO:

  • messy = bagunçado / sujo
  • clean up = limpar / arrumar
  • vacuum cleaner = aspirador de pó
  • hall closet = armário do corredor
  • dirty dishes = louça suja
  • trash = lixo
  • take (tempo) = levar (tempo)

FRASES-CHAVE:

  1. "Why is ___ so messy?" → Por que ___ está tão bagunçado?
  2. "I will clean it right now." → Vou limpar agora mesmo.
  3. "How can I help?" → Como posso ajudar?
  4. "You can ___." → Você pode ___.
  5. "It will take ___ minutes." → Vai levar ___ minutos.

TAREFAS DOMÉSTICAS EM INGLÊS:

  • do the dishes = lavar a louça
  • vacuum the floor = aspirar o chão
  • take out the trash = levar o lixo
  • do the laundry = lavar a roupa
  • make the bed = fazer a cama
  • clean the bathroom = limpar o banheiro

CONVERSA 08 — HOTEL

Tema: Hospedagem, check-in, regras do hotel
Nível: Iniciante-Intermediário
Question words: Where / How much / What / When / How / Can / What time

Q: Where is the hotel located?
A: It is right next to the beach.

Q: How much does a night cost?
A: It costs $80 per night.

Q: What does the price include?

Q: When can we check in?

Q: Can we bring our dog?
A: No, pets are not allowed here.

Q: What time is checkout?

VOCABULÁRIO:

  • located = localizado / situado
  • right next to = bem ao lado de / pertinho de
  • per night = por noite
  • includes = inclui
  • check in = fazer check-in / se hospedar
  • pets = animais de estimação
  • allowed = permitido
  • checkout = check-out / saída

FRASES-CHAVE:

  1. "It is right next to ___." → Fica bem ao lado de ___.
  2. "How much does it cost per night?" → Quanto custa por noite?
  3. "What does the price include?" → O que o preço inclui?
  4. "Pets are not allowed." → Animais não são permitidos.
  5. "Checkout is at ___." → O checkout é às ___.

NO HOTEL — FRASES ESSENCIAIS:

  • "I have a reservation." → Tenho uma reserva.
  • "Can I get a room with a sea view?" → Posso ter um quarto com vista para o mar?
  • "Is breakfast included?" → O café da manhã está incluído?
  • "Can I have a late checkout?" → Posso fazer checkout mais tarde?

CONVERSA 09 — COZINHANDO

Tema: Cozinha, receitas, culinária
Nível: Iniciante
Question words: What / How / Why / When / Where / Can / Is

Q: What are you making for dinner?
A: I am making chicken pasta.

Q: How do you cook the chicken?
A: I fry it with some oil.

Q: Why do you add so much garlic?
A: Because garlic adds a great flavor.

Q: When should I boil the pasta?
A: Boil it when the water is hot.

Q: Where did you find this recipe?
A: I found it on the internet.

Q: Can you bake bread, too?
A: Yes, but it takes more time.

Q: Is the food ready yet?
A: Yes, let's eat right now.

VOCABULÁRIO:

  • chicken pasta = macarrão com frango
  • fry = fritar
  • garlic = alho
  • flavor = sabor / sabor
  • boil = ferver / cozinhar em água
  • recipe = receita
  • bake = assar (no forno)
  • yet = ainda / já

FRASES-CHAVE:

  1. "What are you making?" → O que você está fazendo/cozinhando?
  2. "I fry it with some oil." → Frijo com um pouco de óleo.
  3. "Garlic adds a great flavor." → O alho dá um ótimo sabor.
  4. "It takes more time." → Leva mais tempo.
  5. "Let's eat right now." → Vamos comer agora mesmo.

MÉTODOS DE COZIMENTO:

  • fry = fritar
  • bake = assar (forno)
  • boil = ferver
  • grill = grelhar
  • steam = cozinhar no vapor
  • roast = assar (carne)

INGREDIENTES COMUNS:

  • garlic = alho
  • olive oil = azeite
  • salt and pepper = sal e pimenta
  • onion = cebola
  • herbs = ervas
  • spices = temperos

CONVERSA 10 — TRANSPORTE E DIREÇÕES

Tema: Metrô, ônibus, como chegar a algum lugar
Nível: Iniciante
Question words: Where / How / Can / How long / Where should / Is there / How often

Q: Where is the nearest metro station?
A: It's on Park Avenue.

Q: How do I get there?
A: Turn left and go straight ahead.

Q: Can I walk there from here?
A: Yes, it's 30 minutes on foot.

Q: How long will it take by bus?
A: It should take just around 7 minutes.

Q: Where should I get off?
A: Get off at the Central Park stop.

Q: Is there a bus stop nearby?
A: Yes, there's one on the next street.

Q: How often do the buses come?
A: They come every 15 minutes or so.

VOCABULÁRIO:

  • nearest = mais próximo
  • metro station = estação de metrô
  • go straight ahead = siga em frente
  • on foot = a pé
  • get off = descer (ônibus/metrô)
  • stop = parada / ponto
  • nearby = perto / nas proximidades
  • every 15 minutes or so = a cada 15 minutos mais ou menos

FRASES-CHAVE:

  1. "Where is the nearest ___?" → Onde fica o/a ___ mais próximo/a?
  2. "Turn left and go straight." → Vire à esquerda e siga em frente.
  3. "It's ___ minutes on foot." → São ___ minutos a pé.
  4. "Get off at ___." → Desça em ___.
  5. "They come every ___ minutes." → Passam a cada ___ minutos.

DIREÇÕES — VOCABULÁRIO ESSENCIAL:

  • turn left = vire à esquerda
  • turn right = vire à direita
  • go straight = siga em frente
  • at the corner = na esquina
  • next to = ao lado de
  • across from = em frente a / do outro lado de
  • between = entre

CONVERSA 11 — TEMPO E CLIMA

Tema: Clima, previsão do tempo, o que vestir
Nível: Iniciante-Intermediário
Question words: How's / How cold / Should / Will there / How will / How can / What should

Q: How's the weather?
A: It's cold and a bit cloudy.

Q: How cold is it there?
A: It's about 8 degrees.

Q: Should I bring an umbrella?
A: Yes, it might rain any time.

Q: Will there be thunder and lightning?
A: I can already hear the thunder.

Q: How will the weather be tomorrow?
A: It will probably be sunny and warm.

Q: How can you be sure?
A: I'm not sure. Just guessing.

Q: What should I wear tomorrow?
A: A light jacket should be enough.

VOCABULÁRIO:

  • cloudy = nublado
  • umbrella = guarda-chuva
  • thunder = trovão
  • lightning = relâmpago
  • probably = provavelmente
  • sunny = ensolarado
  • light jacket = jaqueta leve
  • should be enough = deveria ser suficiente
  • just guessing = só chutando

FRASES-CHAVE:

  1. "It's cold and a bit cloudy." → Está frio e um pouco nublado.
  2. "It might rain any time." → Pode chover a qualquer momento.
  3. "It will probably be ___." → Provavelmente vai estar ___.
  4. "I'm not sure. Just guessing." → Não tenho certeza. Só chutando.
  5. "A ___ should be enough." → Um/Uma ___ deveria ser suficiente.

DESCREVENDO O CLIMA:

  • hot = quente / sunny = ensolarado
  • warm = morno / cloudy = nublado
  • cool = fresquinho / windy = ventoso
  • cold = frio / rainy = chuvoso
  • freezing = congelando / snowy = nevando
  • humid = úmido / dry = seco

CONVERSA 12 — VIAGEM E FÉRIAS

Tema: Destinos, experiências de viagem
Nível: Iniciante-Intermediário
Question words: Where / How long / How / What / Was / Did / Would

Q: Where did you go last summer?
A: I went to Thailand.

Q: How long were you there?
A: I was there for two weeks.

Q: How did you get there?
A: I flew from New York.

Q: What did you do there?
A: I visited temples and beaches.

Q: What was the food like?
A: It was spicy but really delicious.

Q: Did you have any problems there?
A: Yes, I lost my phone once.

Q: Would you go back again?
A: Yes, absolutely. I loved it.

VOCABULÁRIO:

  • last summer = no verão passado
  • flew = voei (passado de fly)
  • temples = templos
  • spicy = picante / apimentado
  • delicious = delicioso
  • lost my phone = perdi meu celular
  • absolutely = com certeza / absolutamente

FRASES-CHAVE:

  1. "Where did you go last summer?" → Onde você foi no verão passado?
  2. "I was there for ___ weeks." → Fiquei lá por ___ semanas.
  3. "I flew from ___." → Voei de ___.
  4. "What was the food like?" → Como era a comida?
  5. "Would you go back again?" → Você voltaria?
  6. "Yes, absolutely. I loved it." → Sim, com certeza. Amei.

FALANDO DE VIAGEM:

  • "The trip was amazing." → A viagem foi incrível.
  • "I'd highly recommend it." → Recomendo muito.
  • "The people were very friendly." → O povo era muito simpático.
  • "It's worth the trip." → Vale a viagem.

PERGUNTA ESPECIAL:
"What was ___ like?" → Como era ___? / Como foi ___?
Essa estrutura é muito usada e pouco ensinada!

  • "What was the food like?" → Como era a comida?
  • "What was the hotel like?" → Como era o hotel?
  • "What was he like?" → Como ele era?

CONVERSA 13 — APRENDENDO INGLÊS

Tema: Estudo de idiomas, dificuldades, progresso
Nível: Intermediário
Question words: Why / How long / How often / What / How / Can / Have

Q: Why do you want to learn English?
A: I need it for my job.

Q: How long have you been studying?
A: I've been studying for 2 years.

Q: How often do you practice?
A: I practice every day for an hour.

Q: What do you find most difficult?
A: I find speaking the most difficult.

Q: How do you practice speaking?
A: I watch English shows without subtitles.

Q: Can you speak well now?
A: I can speak fairly well.

Q: Have you taken any English tests?
A: Yes, I took the IELTS last year.

VOCABULÁRIO:

  • need it for my job = preciso para o meu trabalho
  • been studying = tenho estudado (Present Perfect Continuous)
  • practice = praticar
  • find ___ difficult = achar ___ difícil
  • subtitles = legendas
  • fairly well = razoavelmente bem
  • IELTS = exame internacional de inglês

FRASES-CHAVE:

  1. "I need it for my job." → Preciso para o meu trabalho.
  2. "I've been studying for ___." → Estudo há ___.
  3. "I practice every day for ___." → Pratico todo dia por ___.
  4. "I find ___ the most difficult." → Acho ___ o mais difícil.
  5. "I can speak fairly well." → Consigo falar razoavelmente bem.

FALE SOBRE SEU INGLÊS:

  • Why do you want to learn English? → I want to ___ / I need it for ___.
  • How long have you been studying? → I've been studying for ___.
  • What do you find most difficult? → I find ___ the most difficult.
  • How do you practice? → I ___.

ADVÉRBIOS DE GRAU (para descrever seu nível):

  • fluently = fluentemente
  • fairly well = razoavelmente bem
  • a little / a bit = um pouco
  • not very well = não muito bem
  • barely = mal / quase não

CONVERSA 14 — CHEGANDO ATRASADO

Tema: Atraso, desculpas, trânsito, comprometimento
Nível: Iniciante-Intermediário
Question words: Why / When / How / Will / How can / How / What if

Q: Why are you late?
A: I got stuck in traffic.

Q: When did you leave home?
A: I left home at 9 a.m.

Q: How did you come here?
A: I took the bus.

Q: Will you be late again?
A: No, I won't be late ever again.

Q: How can you assure me?
A: Well, I'll leave from home early.

Q: How early?
A: About 30 minutes early.

Q: What if you're late again?
A: You can punish me.

VOCABULÁRIO:

  • got stuck in traffic = fiquei preso no trânsito
  • leave home = sair de casa
  • took the bus = peguei o ônibus
  • ever again = nunca mais
  • assure = assegurar / garantir
  • punish = punir / castigar

FRASES-CHAVE:

  1. "I got stuck in traffic." → Fiquei preso no trânsito.
  2. "I took the bus." → Peguei o ônibus.
  3. "I won't ___ ever again." → Nunca mais vou ___.
  4. "I'll leave from home early." → Vou sair de casa cedo.
  5. "What if ___?" → E se ___?

DESCULPAS EM INGLÊS:

  • "I'm so sorry I'm late." → Sinto muito por estar atrasado.
  • "I got stuck in traffic." → Fiquei preso no trânsito.
  • "My bus was delayed." → Meu ônibus atrasou.
  • "I overslept." → Eu dormi demais.
  • "It won't happen again." → Não vai acontecer de novo.

EXPRESSÃO ESPECIAL:
"I got stuck in ___."

  • stuck in traffic = preso no trânsito
  • stuck in a meeting = preso em uma reunião
  • stuck at work = preso no trabalho
  • stuck in an elevator = preso no elevador
